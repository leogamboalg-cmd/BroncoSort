# BroncoSort Privacy Policy

BroncoSort does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The extension reads publicly available course and instructor information on the Cal Poly Pomona registration website to display professor ratings.

Professor names may be sent to a backend service to retrieve publicly available ratings from RateMyProfessors.

No personal identifiers, browsing history, or sensitive user data are collected or stored.

This extension is not affiliated with or endorsed by Cal Poly Pomona or RateMyProfessors.
