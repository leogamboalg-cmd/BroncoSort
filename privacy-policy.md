# BroncoSort Privacy Policy

BroncoSort respects user privacy and is designed to avoid collecting personal or sensitive user data.

## Information the Extension Uses

BroncoSort reads course, section, and instructor information from supported university registration or schedule pages in order to display professor ratings and sorting features.

This information may include publicly visible course details such as:

* instructor names
* course names or numbers
* section information
* registration table layout information

BroncoSort uses this information only to provide extension functionality.

## Backend Requests

Professor names and related course context may be sent to the BroncoSort backend service to retrieve publicly available professor rating information from RateMyProfessors or cached BroncoSort data.

BroncoSort may also send limited page-structure data to improve compatibility with supported school registration pages. This data is used to help the extension understand how school registration pages are structured.

## School Requests

If a user requests support for a new school through the extension popup, BroncoSort may store the requested school name and related school information.

This is used only to track requested schools and improve future support.

## Information BroncoSort Does Not Collect

BroncoSort does not collect, store, or share:

* names of users
* student ID numbers
* passwords
* emails
* personal identifiers
* grades
* payment information
* sensitive personal information
* full browsing history
* private messages or communications

BroncoSort does not sell user data.

## Data Sharing

BroncoSort does not sell, rent, or share personal user data with advertisers or third parties.

Limited technical requests may be sent to the BroncoSort backend only as needed for extension functionality, such as retrieving professor ratings or processing school support requests.

## Permissions

BroncoSort uses Chrome extension permissions only to run on supported school registration or schedule pages and to display rating-related features.

The extension does not use permissions to track unrelated browsing activity.

## Affiliation Disclaimer

BroncoSort is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Cal Poly Pomona, the California State University system, the University of California system, any supported school, or RateMyProfessors.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or BroncoSort, contact the developer through the project’s GitHub page or Chrome Web Store listing.
