❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Questions,
answered.

BroncoSort helps students choose better professors by bringing RateMyProfessors ratings directly into supported registration pages. Here are answers to the most common questions.

What does BroncoSort do?

BroncoSort adds RateMyProfessors ratings directly into supported registration pages and automatically sorts class sections by highest-rated professors.

Which schools are supported?

Cal Poly Pomona is currently supported. Additional CSU, UC, and community college campuses can request support directly through the extension.

Is BroncoSort free?

Yes. BroncoSort is completely free to install and use.

Does BroncoSort collect personal information?

BroncoSort minimizes data collection and does not collect passwords, payment information, emails, or browsing history outside supported school pages.

Why does the extension need page access?

BroncoSort needs access to supported registration pages so it can detect professor names, display ratings, and sort course sections.

How do school requests work?

Students can request support for unsupported campuses directly inside the extension. Requests help improve compatibility for additional schools.

Is BroncoSort affiliated with RateMyProfessors?

No. BroncoSort uses publicly available professor rating information but is not affiliated with RateMyProfessors.

Why are some professors missing ratings?

Some professors may not have reviews available on RateMyProfessors yet, or their names may not match existing listings.

Having issues or want to suggest a feature?

Feedback helps improve BroncoSort. Please leave feedback through the Chrome Web Store listing or future support channels as the project expands.

More schools are coming.

BroncoSort is expanding support across California universities and community colleges. Students can help prioritize campuses by requesting their school directly through the extension.
