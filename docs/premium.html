Premium questions

How is my purchase connected to my account?

You sign in with Google, BroncoSort verifies the signed ID token on the backend, and Stripe Checkout is created for that verified account.

Does BroncoSort receive my Google password?

No. Google handles the sign-in. BroncoSort only receives a signed identity token that the backend verifies.

When does Premium activate?

Premium activates only after Stripe confirms payment through the signed webhook. The checkout success page does not grant access by itself.
